Curriculum Vitae

Professor Bio

    Dr. Li received a Ph.D. degree from the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2020. During 2019-2021, he was a Research Fellow with the Stevens Institute of Technology, Hoboken, NJ, USA under IEEE Fellow, Prof. Wu Lei. From 2021-2023, he was a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University and Singapore ETH Center under IEEE Fellow, Prof. Wang Peng and Prof. Xu Yan. From April. 2023, Dr. Li joined Aalto University as an Assistant Professor.
    Till Now, he has published 57 journal papers, 13 conference papers, 2 books, and 2 patents. Among them, there are 13 first-authored journal papers of which 10 are top-tier journals, and 6 first-authored conference papers. He got Google Scholar citations 3711 times (h index 31, i10 index: 48) and in total 9 highly cited research papers (7 of which are first-authored journal papers, TOP 1%) and two hot paper (TOP 0.1%). One paper is regarded as pioneering research on the IEEE Innovation Spotlight website. In 2023, he was selected into the World’s Top 2% of Scientists in the subfield of “Energy”. In 2024, he was selected into the World’s Top 0.5% of Scientists in ScholarGPS. In 2025, one of his first author paper won the best paper award among 2000+ papers from IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid. He won the best reviewer of the journals IEEE Transactions on Power Systems, IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy(twice), CSEE JPES, and IET Economic Conversion Management, and the best editorial award from IET ECE.
    Dr. Li serves as an associate editor and guest editor of IEEE Transactions on Sustainable Energy, IEEE Transactions on Industry Application, IEEE Access, Scientific Reports, Journal of Energy Storage, IET Renewable Power Generation, Heliyon (SCI Journal under cell), Protection and Control of Modern Power Systems, Frontiers in Energy Research, and IET Economic Conversion Management journals. He has successfully applied for more than 10 special issues and conference topics.
